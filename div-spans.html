Everything about shark

Here is everything you need to know about shark. First, the important stuff:

There are 537 shark species, divided between 8 orders.

Here is types of sharks

  • Tiger shark
  • blue shark
  • mackerel shark
  • great white shark

Sharks are fishes and most have the typical fusiform body shape. Like other fishes, sharks are ectothermic (cold-blooded), live in water, have fins, and breathe with gills.

The largest shark is the 13.7 m (45 ft) whale shark. The 22 to 25 cm (8.7 to 9.8 in.) midwater shark and pygmy ribbontail catshark are among the smallest.
